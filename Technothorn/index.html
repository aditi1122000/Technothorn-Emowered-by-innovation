Our thought process

To cut down the hustle of travelling, we planned for an ultra-compact personal aerial vehicle (PAV) with novel technologies applied in a very practical fashion. SAR2 is a dual mode; one modal structure which provides the best experience both on land and in air.

Features of SAR2

Real time object detection

SAR2 provides object detection with fast inferencing in real time. This will also help us to understand and analyze scenes in captured images or videos.

AI powered Navigation system

Smarter way of navigating and cluster controlling. Also seeks to provide temperature meter of the current location.

Virtual assistant

An intelligent agent which will assist the driver by helping him in answering simple queries and performing routined tasks.

  • Responsive Design Specialists

    Real-Time Network Connection: The technology of autonomous flight eliminates the possibility of failure or malfunction caused by man-made errors. Flight routes will be surveyed in advance to preset multiple feasible plans for the users.

    BMS (Battery Management System): The charging devices can communicate in real-time with the aircraft Battery Management System (BMS).

    Comprehensive Management by Smart Control: The control system provides core functions of monitoring, dispatching, controlling, early-warning and cluster management of the aerial vehicle.

  • Safety is our prior service

    PAV Safety: Redundancy design for all major flight components to ensure flight safety.

    Fail-Safe System: The Fail-Safe safety system serves as a reliable guardian by equipping itself with multiple-redundant protection plans

    Flight Testing and Quality Assurance: Testing subjects include static test, load ground test, load flight test, durability test, reliability test, environmental test, anti-interference test etc. Under all kinds of inclement environments such as high/low temperature, high humidity, salt spray, typhoon, rainstorm the aerial vehicle will be tested to ensure total safety.
